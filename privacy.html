Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to me. This policy outlines how I handle your information when you visit lewiswake.com.

1. No Tracking or Analytics

I respect your digital privacy. This website does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics software (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) to monitor your behavior. I do not collect data on your location, device type, or browsing habits.

2. External Links

This website serves primarily as a hub to link you to my content on other platforms (such as YouTube, Patreon, Bandcamp, etc.). Once you click these links and leave lewiswake.com, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective third-party websites.

3. Contact Form & Personal Data

If you use the contact form on this website to get in touch regarding bookings or inquiries:

  • Data Collected: I collect only the information you voluntarily provide (Name, Email Address, and Message).
  • Purpose: This information is used solely to reply to your inquiry.
  • Confidentiality: Your details are strictly confidential between myself and the sender. I will never sell, share, or distribute your email address to third parties or marketing lists.

4. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me via the form on the homepage.
